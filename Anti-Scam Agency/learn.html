Understanding Scams

Scams come in many forms and can be difficult to identify. Below are some common types of scams and how to protect yourself from them:

  • Phishing: Fraudulent attempts to obtain sensitive information.
  • Identity Theft: The theft of personal information to commit fraud.
  • Investment Scams: Promises of high returns with little risk.
  • Online Shopping Fraud: Fake websites or sellers that don’t deliver goods.
