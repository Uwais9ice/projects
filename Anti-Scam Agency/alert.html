Scam Alerts

Phishing Email Scam

Be cautious of emails claiming to be from well-known organizations asking for personal information. Always verify the source before taking any action.

Credit Card Fraud

Watch out for unauthorized transactions on your credit card. Report any suspicious activity immediately to your bank.

Password Reset Scam

Be wary of unsolicited password reset requests. Always ensure you are on the official website before entering any sensitive information.

Phone Scams

Avoid providing personal information over the phone unless you are certain of the caller's identity. Hang up and call back through official channels if in doubt.

Prize Scams

Do not fall for schemes that promise large sums of money or valuable prizes in exchange for a small fee or personal information.
