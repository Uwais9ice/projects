Who we are

The National Association of State Credit Unions (NASC) is a key organization dedicated to supporting and representing state-chartered credit unions across the United States. NASC is responsible for advocating on behalf of its members, providing essential resources and educational opportunities, and influencing public policy to promote a strong and effective credit union sector. The organization plays a crucial role in fostering a collaborative environment for state credit unions, ensuring they have the tools and support needed to thrive in a competitive financial landscape.

Our Mandate

NASC is charged with the responsibility of advancing the interests of state-chartered credit unions through effective advocacy, education, and resource provision. Our mandate includes overseeing the development and implementation of policies that impact state credit unions, supporting members with valuable insights and training, and ensuring that best practices are adopted across the sector. NASC works diligently to address the evolving needs of credit unions, ensuring they remain competitive and capable of delivering exceptional service to their members.

Our Mission

To enhance the success and growth of state-chartered credit unions by providing comprehensive advocacy, education, and resources, while promoting a collaborative and innovative environment that supports the financial well-being of our members and the communities they serve.

Our Vision

To be the leading voice for state credit unions in the United States, driving innovation, influencing policy, and fostering a cooperative spirit that strengthens the credit union movement and contributes to the economic stability and prosperity of the communities we serve.

About Us

We are a leading company in our industry, committed to delivering high-quality products and services to our customers. Our mission is to provide exceptional value and service with a focus on innovation and excellence.
